How to initiate contact

Where a direct email/mobile is verified (Clay Craft, RNS, Bharat, BAFA, DNF, Hitkari, Mulder, K.B. Polychem) — email direct; it reaches a sales manager faster than the marketplace. Where contact only lives on IndiaMART (Fienza, India Ceramic, HandiGlobal, Surabhi) — use the “Get Best Price” / “Contact Supplier” button; the seller's number is revealed and your lead forwarded. Numbers starting 8062… / 079 4267… are IndiaMART call-tracking lines — once connected, ask for a direct WhatsApp/mobile.

First email should state: our brand, that we design in-house and need OEM/private-label production, the product & quality tier, whether we need custom shapes/moulds vs decal-only, indicative annual volume, and ask for catalogue + price list + MOQ.

What to ask on the first call

  • MOQ per SKU and per colour/decal design — Khurja/Jaipur handmade typically ~500–1,000 pcs per shape; bone-china & Morbi often higher.
  • Custom moulds: one-time tooling cost + lead time (new shape moulds ~4–8 weeks) vs decorating their existing bodies with our decal (much faster).
  • Samples ~10–15 days; bulk 30–60 days after design sign-off.
  • Food-safe: lead-free / cadmium-free certification on decals & glazes (ask K.B. Polychem / Mulder to confirm).
  • Pricing basis: ex-works vs FOB, retail vs bulk packaging, payment terms (advance % + balance).
  • Capacity & consistency: monthly capacity, breakage/reject policy, and whether they'll hold our moulds/decals exclusively.
