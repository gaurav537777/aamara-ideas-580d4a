Chapter 7 · The Moat

The margin is the design, not the clay.

A plate costs about ₹200 to make and sells at about ₹1,000. That gap isn't markup for its own sake — it's the design, the monogram and the box. Own those and the moat is yours; the kiln is a vendor line item.

That ~5× is Nicobar's proven band, not a hope. The clay is commoditised and outsourced; design + packaging + brand are the only things a competitor can't copy — so they're the only things we keep in-house. EST.
