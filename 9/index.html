One conflict, one empty box.

Chapter 6 · The Human Number

The most important ₹ in the deck.

The moat isn't a feature — it's an incentive. A rental operator earns nothing from a home that sits safely empty; their living depends on renting it out. This business earns its living from exactly the opposite: keeping the asset protected whether it's ever let or not.
