Chapter 6 · The Economics

Premium is a thin slice, not the middle.

The gap between the median fee and the premium band isn't nicer sofas — it's ratios, safety and trust. That's the only gap worth building in.

Rent dominates the P&L (₹3–6L/month for prime ground-floor + play area); break-even is roughly 55–75 children; fees are paid upfront, giving strong negative working capital once full. The two things that break it: a bad lease, or a single trust incident. EST.
