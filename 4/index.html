Chapter 6 · The Human Number

The most unprotected pet in the room.

The moat isn't just an app — it's a health record. Pet insurance penetration in India is below 1%, versus 25%+ in the UK and 40%+ in Sweden. Most pet spend here is out-of-pocket, which forces families into cost-based medical decisions for an animal they treat as family.

That sub-1% is inflecting — growing ~19–20% CAGR, with new entrants like Universal Sompo and Future Generali writing ~25,000 policies in a single quarter. A continuously-updated pet health record positions this chain as a future insurer-distribution partner — turning the data moat into real financial protection for a family's pet, not just a retention hook.
